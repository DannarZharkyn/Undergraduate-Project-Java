The Problem

One pillow doesn't fit everyone.

Many people wake up with neck pain, stiffness, or poor sleep because pillow height and support are highly personal. Yet most pillows are still sold as one-size-fits-all products.

Our Vision

Sleep support that adapts to you.

Somni Systems is developing adjustable sleep solutions that allow people to personalize their support instead of constantly replacing pillows.

Why We're Building This

Built from real conversations.

After speaking with physical therapists, sleep specialists, and people struggling with neck discomfort, we repeatedly heard the same thing: pillow height matters, and there is no one-size-fits-all solution.
