The mismatch

Traditional pillows stay the same. Your body doesn’t.

So you do the adapting.

Stacking. Folding. Flipping. Buying another pillow and hoping it works. We treat these workarounds as normal because the pillow itself cannot respond when our position—and the support we want—changes.

Meet Somni

The pillow should do the adapting.

Somni is an adjustable pillow concept built around a simple principle: support should be able to change when your body does.

  • ✓ Change the height and support
  • ✓ Reconfigure it as your needs change
  • ✓ Replace workarounds with one system

Why trust the idea

Built from the workaround backward.

We’re not starting with another pillow and inventing a story around it. We’re starting with the behavior: how people stack, fold, replace, and reshape their pillows. Customer interviews, physical therapist conversations, prototypes, and user testing are turning that insight into a product.
